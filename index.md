# Group Page & Personas

## Project
**Project name:** _Night at Maple Bridge: An Interactive Cultural Guide to Suzhou Maple Bridge_  
**Short description:** Night at Maple Bridge” is an interactive cultural guide website centered on Maple Bridge in Suzhou. Designed for tourists, culture enthusiasts, and first-time visitors, the website provides attraction introductions, route recommendations, poem-based interactive navigation, night tour suggestions, and nearby travel information.

## Personas (10–80–10 Rule)

### Persona 1 (Represents the **80% mainstream** users)
**Name:** Alex Chen  
**Age:** 20  
**Background:** Undergraduate student, busy schedule, uses phone + laptop daily.  
**Goals:**
- Complete tasks quickly with minimal learning cost
- Avoid mistakes and confusion
**Behaviors:**
- Skims instructions; prefers clear buttons and defaults
- Uses the product in short sessions (2–5 minutes)
**Pain points:**
- Too many steps or unclear labels
- Hard-to-find key actions
**Accessibility/Context:**
- Often uses the product in noisy environments; needs clear visual hierarchy
**Key needs (design requirements):**
- Simple primary flow; consistent navigation; strong feedback (success/error)
